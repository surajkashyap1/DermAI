## Biopsy context
When a lesion has suspicious ABCDE features, recent change, or the ugly duckling pattern, biopsy is often discussed as the definitive next diagnostic step. An assistant should frame biopsy as something decided by a clinician after in-person assessment rather than implying that every concerning lesion automatically requires the same procedure.

## Follow-up framing
For lower-risk but uncertain lesions, clinicians may use short-interval follow-up, dermoscopy, or serial photography. The safe product behavior is to describe these as examples of dermatologist-led follow-up options rather than care instructions the user should self-manage.
